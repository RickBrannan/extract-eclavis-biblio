Epistles of Ignatius to Mary and John

Standard abbreviation: Ep. Ign. John Mary

Other titles: Correspondence of Ignatius with John the Evangelist and the Virgin Mary

Clavis numbers: ECCA 691

Category: Epistles

Related literature: Mary’s letters to the people of Messina and Florence

Status: in progress by J. Gregory Given

Citing this resource (using Chicago Manual of Style): Given, J. Gregory. “Epistles of Ignatius to Mary and John.” e-Clavis: Christian Apocrypha. Accessed DAY MONTH YEAR. https://www.nasscal.com/e-clavis-christian-apocrypha/epistles-of-ignatius-to-mary-and-john/.

Created September 2022.

1. SUMMARY

Named Historical Figures and Characters: Anna (mother of Mary), Ignatius, Jesus Christ, John (son of Zebedee), Mary (Virgin), Salome (daughter of Anna).

Geographical Locations: Jerusalem.
